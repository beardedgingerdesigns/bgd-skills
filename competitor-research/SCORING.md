# Scoring Matrix

Score each site out of 100. Apply every criterion; zero for missing.

## First Impression (25 pts)

| Criterion | Points | What to check |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Clear value prop above fold | 10 | Can you tell what they do in 5 seconds? |
| Visual hierarchy | 8 | Does the eye flow naturally? Or is it cluttered? |
| Professional typography | 7 | Consistent font system, readable sizes, proper spacing |

## Conversion Architecture (25 pts)

| Criterion | Points | What to check |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Primary CTA visible without scroll | 8 | Button above fold, clear action verb |
| CTA repetition | 5 | CTA appears at least 3x on the page |
| Social proof near CTA | 7 | Testimonial, stat, or logo row within one scroll of a CTA |
| Clear next step | 5 | Visitor always knows what to do next |

## Content Strategy (20 pts)

| Criterion | Points | What to check |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Benefit-led copy | 7 | Headlines state outcomes, not features |
| Scannable structure | 5 | Headers, short paragraphs, bullet points |
| Real imagery | 5 | Product photos, team photos, or custom assets (not stock) |
| Content depth | 3 | Enough substance to build trust without overwhelming |

## Trust & Authority (15 pts)

| Criterion | Points | What to check |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Testimonials or case studies | 5 | Named people, specific results |
| Client/partner logos | 4 | Recognizable names or quantity |
| Credentials or awards | 3 | Certifications, press mentions, industry affiliations |
| Contact accessibility | 3 | Phone, address, or chat visible — not buried |

## Technical Execution (15 pts)

| Criterion | Points | What to check |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Mobile responsive | 5 | Layout adapts cleanly |
| Load speed perception | 4 | Does it feel fast? Lazy loading, no layout shift |
| Navigation clarity | 3 | Can you find any page in 2 clicks? |
| Animation/interaction quality | 3 | Smooth and purposeful, or janky/distracting? |
